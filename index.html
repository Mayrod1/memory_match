Silicon Valley Match!

As the CFO of Pied Peipert, you are tasked with raising $1,000,000 to get middle-out compression technology off the ground. Match the characters and the raise capital!

Silicon Valley Match is a concentration game themed after the hit HBO show silicon valley. You get three rounds to match all the characters and raise enough capital to hit your goal before tech-crunch disrupt. You can change the intensity of the game if you would like more of a challenge.
